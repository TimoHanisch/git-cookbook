# Miso Curry 4-6 Portions
This is a non spicy fusion recipe of miso + thai curry style cusine

## Ingredients
* 2 red bell peppers
* 200g of mushrooms
* 400g of chicken breast
* 2 red onions
* 400ml of coconut milk
* 1 glove of garlic
* 2 Tsps of miso paste
* 2 Tsps of mixed curry powder
* 1 Tbsp of soy sauce
* 3 Tbsps of oil
